Our Loyalty Scheme

EAT.

Here at CJ's, we welcome our guests to enjoy our mouth-watering American cuisine.

Whether you're a meat-lover, a vegetarian or a vegan, you'll find something from our menu to satisfy your tastebuds.

EARN.

We believe in giving back to our loyal customers, so as a thank you, you will be rewarded points for dining with us across our restaurants nationwide.

For every £5 spent, you will receive 1 point

REDEEM.

  • Collect 5 points for a FREE milkshake or non-alcoholic beverage
  • Collect 15 points for a FREE starter or dessert
  • Collect 25 points for a FREE main and a choice of starter or dessert

THE SMALL PRINT.

  • There is no maximum number of points you can earn per dining experience so the more you spend, the more goods you will receive.
  • Points expire after 12 months and are non-transferable to other people. To redeem the free rewards, the points-holder must be present.
  • Food is subject to availability within our restaurants nationwide - don't worry, we do our best to keep favourites in stock.
